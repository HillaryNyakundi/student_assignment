CS100 Discussion Questions

What are the ethical considerations surrounding the use of public Wi-Fi networks, and how can individuals protect their data when connecting to such networks?

Public Wi-Fi networks often lack encryption, posing data privacy risks.Users should respect network policies and avoid illegal or harmful activities while using these networks.

To protect their data, individuals should use VPNs, avoid accessing sensitive information on public networks, and verify secure website connections. Taking these precautions ensures safer internet usage in public spaces.

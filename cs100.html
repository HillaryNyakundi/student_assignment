CS100 Discussion Questions

How can individuals develop their digital literacy skills, and what are the benefits of being digitally literate in
today's society?

Compare and contrast the physical health risks and behavioral health risks associated with prolonged computer
use. How can individuals mitigate these risks?

Describe the World Wide Web. Identify reasons people use the World Wide Web.

What are the ethical considerations surrounding the use of public Wi-Fi networks, and how can individuals
protect their data when connecting to such networks?

Discuss the role of multi-factor authentication (MFA) in enhancing digital security. How does MFA work, and why
is it important for online accounts?

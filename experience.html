Envisioned design description:

The prototype showcased the homepage of my website. The design included a header section with a title, an image of myself, and a brief introduction paragraph. Below that, there is an unordered list of links to other pages, and a centered footer at the bottom. The layout is clean and simple, with minimal colors to keep the focus on the content.

Transformation experiences:

I managed to tweak the wireframes of the design, I had chosen illustrations as the images to disguise myself but had challenges blending them with the typograpy and background of the page as the were initially drawn, instead i used a car image, fixed the unordered list, the introductory paragraph fitted nicely, the layout was clean and simple, i experienced the power of CSS in this tranformation.

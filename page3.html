CS100 Discussion Questions

Describe the World Wide Web. Identify reasons people use the World Wide Web.

The World Wide Web (WWW) is a system of interlinked hypertext documents that can be accessed through the internet using a web browser. It provides access to vast information and online services.

People use the World Wide Web for information gathering, communication, commerce, entertainment, and collaboration. Its wide range of services has made it essential in daily life.

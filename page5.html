CS100 Discussion Questions

Describe the concept of “phishing” in the context of digital security. What are some effective strategies to recognize and prevent phishing attacks?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers impersonate legitimate entities to steal sensitive information like passwords or credit card details, often through fraudulent emails or websites.

To prevent phishing, users should verify email senders, check for secure website protocols (HTTPS), and avoid clicking suspicious links. Anti-phishing software also helps protect against such attacks.

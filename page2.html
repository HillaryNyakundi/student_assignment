CS100 Discussion Questions

Compare and contrast the physical health risks and behavioral health risks associated with prolonged computer use. How can individuals mitigate these risks?

Prolonged computer use can cause physical health issues like eye strain, back pain, and repetitive strain injuries, while also leading to behavioral health problems such as internet addiction and social isolation.

To mitigate these risks, individuals should take regular breaks, set up ergonomic workspaces, and manage screen time mindfully. Physical activity and proper posture are also key to preventing health problems.

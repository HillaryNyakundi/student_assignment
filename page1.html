CS100 Discussion Questions

How can individuals develop their digital literacy skills, and what are the benefits of being digitally literate in today's society?

Individuals can develop their digital literacy skills by taking online courses, exploring digital platforms, attending tech workshops, and practicing responsible technology use. These actions enhance the ability to navigate a digital world confidently.

The benefits include improved career prospects, enhanced communication, better access to information, and participation in the digital economy. Digital literacy is key to thriving in today’s society.

Drawing on the study of different cities in the Global South, the intensive use of data changes politics, power relations, and everyday life in contemporary cities.
